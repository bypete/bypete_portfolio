## Agile environments
I have worked in collaborative environments with back-end developers, translators and stakeholders. With [OnePay](/work/onepay/), a Visa prepaid card for temporary employees, I directly managed the supplied translation content for six languages and populated it into the Umbraco CMS as it became available. 

Working closely with the client, and drawing on my experience within the prepaid sector, I was able to accommodate their ideas and introduce some key revisions to improve the customer experience.

The static templates were then handed over to a team of back-end developers, complete with detailed annotations, listing indexes, and support documentation.

I assisted in configuring the content management system (Umbraco) and took responsibility for the initial population of all launch content.

I have also worked on regionalised campaigns and understand the specific tailoring they require. For the [K Ring](/work/mykring/) product, I curated a listing index of all the prototyped screens with working navigation.

Any screen could be directly accessed by developers, partner clients and stakeholders. This was especially useful for translators, who often need more context to create concise translations as some screens can only be accessed under certain conditions, which can be difficult and sometimes impossible for third parties to recreate.
